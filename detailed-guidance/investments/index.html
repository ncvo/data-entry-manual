Investment asset breakdown#

We are also looking for a further breakdown of investment assets. This should be found in a note to the accounts, with the note referenced next to the item in the balance sheet. The item should include details of the types of investment - cash, listed, unlisted.

These items should be recorded underneath the main investment item in the "assets and funds" section.
